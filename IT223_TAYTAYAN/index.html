The obsersever

A observer is a state-of-the-art spacecraft designed to explore the alpha centauri

The mission of The Observer is to journey to the Alpha Centauri star system to collect detailed scientific data and expand human knowledge beyond the Solar System.

Recent studies suggest that asteroids and comets from Alpha Centauri may occasionally drift into our solar system, making them "interstellar visitors."
